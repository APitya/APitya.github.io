Me

I am currently in LIS at Aix-Marseille Université, where I am a postdoctoral researcher under Karoliina Lehtinen. Previously, I was a PhD student at the University of Warwick, under the supervision of Marcin Jurdziński. I am broadly interested in everything automata and games, particularly those that involve parity conditions or counters.
