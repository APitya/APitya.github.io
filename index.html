Me

I am a PhD student at the University of Warwick, under the supervision of Marcin Jurdzinski. I am broadly interested in everything automata and games, with an affinity towards those that involve parity conditions or counters. For my PhD, I am researching history-determinism on various models, with a focus on parity automata.
